About HairGenetic

We care about the experience of all women

We are all so unique and there for our hair care should cater to this. Your hair is as unique as your DNA you share traits with other people yet you are different. Did you know that you can have up to three different hair types on one scalp? This is not something that is addressed by the main stream. This can be a journey to understanding your heritage and treat breakage, dry scalp, slow hair growth and change your hair style where you may have thought it impossible!

We are acting in response to diversity in hair textures, styles and maintenance. We help you express yourself by testing your hair, teaching you about how to have beautiful healthy hair and recommend bespoke products and treatments that cater to YOU.
