About Us

Hair is made from a protein called keratin, which contains a whopping 21 different amino acids. Environmental aggressors such as sun and pollution, combined with blow drying, colouring and styling, compromise these amino acid levels causing weakness, split-ends and increased risk of breakage. We can minimise this damage by nourishing and protecting our strands with ingredients such as panthenol, essential oils, algae extracts, wheatgerm and silk proteins.
